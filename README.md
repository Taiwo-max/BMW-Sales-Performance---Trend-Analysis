# BMW Global Revenue Analysis Dashboard (2010–2024)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive revenue analysis dashboard built using historical BMW sales data from 2010 to 2024. The dashboard provides insights into long-term revenue trends, top-performing vehicle models, and regional revenue distribution to support strategic, data-driven decision-making.

The project was completed as a team-based analytics assignment, with each team member responsible for specific analytical components. My primary focus was on the revenue analysis, including trend identification, KPI development, and visualization design related to revenue performance.

## Business Objectives

- Analyze BMW’s global revenue performance over time

- Identify top revenue-generating vehicle models

- Examine revenue distribution across regions

- Highlight key revenue trends, peaks, and dips
## Dashboard Features
### Revenue KPIs

- Total Revenue (2010–2024)

- 2024 Total Revenue & Year-over-Year Change

- Top Revenue-Generating Model

- Highest Revenue Region

## Revenue Analysis Visuals

- Annual Revenue Trend (2010–2024)
  Highlighting major shifts in revenue performance

- Revenue by Model
  Comparison of total revenue across BMW model lines

- Revenue by Region
  Regional breakdown of revenue by model using a heatmap

-- Annotations were added to highlight notable periods such as post-pandemic recovery (2022) and revenue softening (2023) to provide business context.

## Key Insights

- BMW generated approximately $19T in total revenue between 2010 and 2024

- Revenue peaked in 2022, driven by strong post-pandemic demand and premium model performance

- A revenue dip in 2023 suggests market normalization and inflationary pressures

- The 7 Series emerged as the highest revenue-generating model

- Asia was the strongest revenue-contributing region over the period analyzed
